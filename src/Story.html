Our story

Nestled in the heart of our town lies Navajo Bites, a cherished family-owned restaurant steeped in the rich flavors of Navajo cuisine. Founded by Grandma Nizhoni and lovingly upheld by her grandchildren, Sarah and Ben, alongside their parents, Navajo Bites is more than just a dining destination; it's a cultural experience. From savory Navajo tacos to hearty mutton stew, each dish tells a story of heritage and love, drawing in locals and travelers alike. Here, the aroma of sizzling frybread and spices mingles with the welcoming embrace of family, where every customer is treated like kin. At Navajo Bites, tradition meets innovation, and every meal is a celebration of culture and connection. So come, join us for a taste of history, a bite of tradition, and a warm, hearty meal that's made with pride and served with love.
